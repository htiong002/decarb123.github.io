How We Work

Patient capital, clear structure

We focus on the roles a holding company is best placed to play — allocating capital, structuring partnerships, and supporting governance — while operating entities lead in the field.

1

Capital with conviction

We take concentrated positions in ventures where we have genuine conviction about the team, market and long-term trajectory. We are not a diversified fund.

2

Governance and alignment

Decarb123 works closely with portfolio entities on governance design, incentive structures and strategic priorities — not just at entry, but throughout the relationship.

3

Partnership over transaction

We are most useful to partners who value a long-term, collaborative relationship. We are not a transactional capital provider looking for a quick exit.

4

Platform thinking

New ventures and tools benefit from shared brand authority, infrastructure and network under the Decarb123 umbrella from day one.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Decarb123 a consulting firm?

No. Decarb123 is a private investment holding company. Consulting delivery and project execution sit with portfolio entities and implementation partners, not the holding company.

What does Decarb123 actually do?

We allocate capital, support governance, structure strategic partnerships and help portfolio entities create long-term value — particularly in circular economy and low-carbon sectors.

Who should contact Decarb123 directly?

Investors, potential portfolio partners, co-investors and aligned collaborators. Operational or project-level enquiries should be directed to the relevant portfolio entity.

What is this website for?

This is the institutional reference point for Decarb123 Pte Ltd — providing context on our role, portfolio and approach for partners and counterparties.
