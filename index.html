Investment focus

A holding company for real-world sustainability ventures

Decarb123 focuses on circular economy, waste-to-resource and low-carbon models that can be deployed in the real economy across Southeast Asia.

Our role

Decarb123 Pte Ltd is a private investment holding company incorporated in Singapore in 2024. We do not operate as a consulting firm under the Decarb123 name.

Instead, we hold equity interests, provide governance oversight and help structure capital and partnerships around a focused set of sustainability ventures.

  • Incubation and ownership of sustainability-linked ventures.
  • Strategic input on business models, capital structure and partnerships.
  • Oversight and governance at the holding-company level.

Not an operating arm

Operational consulting, project delivery and day-to-day execution are carried out by portfolio entities and implementation partners, not by Decarb123 itself.

This separation keeps our role focused, transparent and free from delivery conflicts, while allowing portfolio companies to build their own brand and capabilities.

  • Portfolio companies lead on client work and implementation.
  • Decarb123 remains lean, long-term and selective.
  • New opportunities are evaluated conservatively and confidentially.

What we look for

We selectively engage with ventures that meet three simple tests:

  • Measurable environmental impact — in circularity, carbon reduction or resource recovery.
  • Operators with technical depth and demonstrated ability to execute projects in the field.
  • Commercial models with a credible path to sustainable scale in Southeast Asia.

How we work

Quiet, hands-on and long-term

Our approach is to stay close enough to matter, and far enough away to let operators operate.

  • Selective entry. We prefer to work with a small number of ventures where our involvement is clearly additive and aligned with long-term sustainability outcomes.
  • Aligned incentives. We look for structures where founders, operating partners and Decarb123 share a common view of value creation and impact, not just short-term revenue.
  • Structured governance. We focus on boards, reporting lines and decision rights that keep ventures accountable without suffocating innovation.
  • Real-world validation. Preference is given to ventures with tangible projects, paying customers or credible pathways to deployment rather than purely conceptual propositions.

What we are not

Decarb123 is not a fund manager, public vehicle or advisory shop. We do not solicit capital from the public, and we do not market consulting services under the Decarb123 name.

All operational engagements, client-facing work and project delivery are run through portfolio companies and implementation partners. This site is a high-level overview of Decarb123’s role as an investment holding company only.

What type of ventures does Decarb123 support?

Decarb123 focuses on circular economy, waste-to-resource and low-carbon models that can be deployed in the real economy. We look for ventures with measurable environmental outcomes and a credible path to sustainable scale in Southeast Asia.

Does Decarb123 operate as a consulting firm?

No. Decarb123 is a private investment holding company. Any consulting, advisory or project delivery work is carried out by portfolio entities and implementation partners, not by Decarb123 itself.

Does Decarb123 accept external investors?

Decarb123 does not market investment products or solicit capital from the public. Engagements are conducted on a direct, bilateral basis with relevant counterparties.

Potential partners typically engage through existing portfolio entities or via direct email. We review opportunities selectively and respond where there is a clear fit with our focus areas and approach.

No. This site is informational only. It provides context on Decarb123’s role as a holding company and its general areas of interest, rather than marketing any specific deal, transaction or mandate.
