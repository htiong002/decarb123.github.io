Investment Holding & Sustainability Ventures

Backing circular economy, low-carbon and sustainability ventures across Southeast Asia.

Decarb123 Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based investment holding company focused on founders and ventures that turn waste into value, enable circular systems, and deliver measurable climate and social impact.

Our Role

We are not an operational consulting business. Operational delivery sits with portfolio companies and partners. Decarb123 provides strategy, governance, networks, and capital at the holding-company level.

Focus Areas

Circular economy, waste-to-resource innovation, low-carbon technology pilots, youth sustainability programmes, and impact-driven ventures across Southeast Asia.

Portfolio

Decarb123 currently holds interests in a small but growing portfolio of sustainability-focused ventures, with a primary emphasis on circular systems, waste-to-resource solutions, and youth-centric climate action.

Circular Transition Partners (CTP)

Sustainability consulting, feasibility design and funding support

Role: 30% shareholder.
Focus: Strategy, feasibility studies, grant-funded projects and public-private partnerships in Singapore, Malaysia and Laos.

LoopX (EcoSense × Decarb123)

Recycling marketplace and climate innovation platform

Role: 30% shareholder.
Highlights: Singapore National Champion at ClimateLaunchpad 2025. Builds digital infrastructure for plastics recycling and circular flows.

WhatAWaste™

Zero-waste and youth engagement initiative

A programme concept focused on engaging students and communities in practical, fun and measurable waste reduction and recycling activities. Decarb123 acts as strategic creator and advisor.

YSAP – Youth Sustainability Ambassador Programme

Youth environmental leadership

Co-developed with partners such as NYAA to nurture youth leaders who can champion sustainability in schools, communities and workplaces.

Project 23 (Laos Biochar Pilot)

Waste-to-biochar and regenerative agriculture

Partners: Link Capital and the Ministry of Agriculture & Forestry (Laos).
Role: Strategic feasibility partner via Circular Transition Partners, exploring cassava waste-to-biochar, carbon credits and farmer upskilling.

Other Early-Stage Collaborations

Textile circularity pilots, waste-to-X innovation concepts, and sustainable agritech models across the region. These are evaluated and structured on a case-by-case basis.

About Decarb123

Decarb123 Pte Ltd is a private investment holding company incorporated in Singapore in 2024. We back ventures that connect commercial viability with measurable environmental and social outcomes.

Who We Are

Decarb123 provides a platform for incubation, ownership and governance of sustainability-focused ventures. We typically work alongside founders and technical partners to de-risk early-stage ideas, structure pilots, and prepare for scale.

Operational consulting and project execution are carried out by portfolio entities such as Circular Transition Partners, not by Decarb123 itself.
